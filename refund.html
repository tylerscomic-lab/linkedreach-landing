✓ 14-day money-back guarantee, no questions asked

1. 14-day money-back guarantee

If you're not happy with a SwiftLayer Pro subscription for any reason, we'll refund it in full — no questions asked — as long as you request the refund within 14 days of your original purchase or most recent renewal charge. This applies to every Pro subscription across the SwiftLayer portfolio, including LinkedReach, CraigsAlert, EtsyAlert, DepopAlert, GrailAlert, ScamDetect Pro, HostCalc Pro, YelpSort Pro, LocalDen, and PropGrade.

2. How to request a refund

1

Email tylerscomic@gmail.com within 14 days of your charge.

2

Include the email address you used at checkout so we can locate your order.

3

We'll confirm and process your refund — no explanation required.

3. Processing time

Once approved, refunds are issued to your original payment method via Lemon Squeezy and typically appear within 5–10 business days, depending on your bank or card issuer.

4. Cancelling your subscription

You don't need a refund to stop being billed — you can cancel any subscription at any time from the Lemon Squeezy customer portal (the link is included in your purchase confirmation and every receipt email). Cancelling stops future renewal charges immediately; you'll keep Pro access through the end of the billing period you already paid for.

5. After 14 days

Requests made after the 14-day window are reviewed on a case-by-case basis. Email us and we'll do our best to work something out, though a refund is not guaranteed outside the guarantee period.
