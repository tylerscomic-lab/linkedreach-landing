How it works

Up and running in 60 seconds.

No account needed. No credit card. Just install, add your free Groq key, and you're done.

1

Install LinkedReach

Add the extension for free from the Chrome Web Store. It's a one-click install with no permissions beyond LinkedIn.com.

2

Get your free Groq API key

Head to console.groq.com, create an account in 30 seconds (no credit card), and copy your API key into the extension settings.

3

Open any LinkedIn profile → Generate

Browse to any linkedin.com/in/* page, click the ⚡ AI Outreach button, choose Connect or Follow-Up, and hit Generate. Done.
